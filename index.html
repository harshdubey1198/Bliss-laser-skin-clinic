About Us

Dr. Shweta Ahuja's
Bliss Aesthetic Skin Laser Clinic

Bliss Aesthetic Skin Laser Clinic is a cosmetic medical practice with experienced practitioners dedicated to providing clients with the best possible care. We specialize in unique, developed and provided by our expert doctors, provided in a warm and friendly environment.

Dr Shweta Ahuja is a well-known celebrity aesthetician and a pioneer in the field of aesthetic in the region. She is one of the leading experts in aesthetic medicine. With over 10 years of experience in beautifying people,her deep understanding of lasers and her passion for her work has made her a well known figure amongst various celebrity clients. She has perfected thousands of beautiful faces and helped her patients rediscover their natural beauty. She deals in all skin related diseases with special interest in nonsurgical facelift procedures.
